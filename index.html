PLDT Innolab & Vitro Data Center

Details

The tour began with a presentation at PLDT Innolab discussing telecommunications infrastructure, innovation, and digital services in the Philippines.

We then visited the Vitro Data Center, where we learned about security, redundancy, monitoring systems, and the importance of reliability in enterprise IT environments.

Takeaways

  • I learned about how they emphasize that REDUNDANCY is a must and failover strategies in enterprise data centers.
  • Importance of monitoring and alerting for uptime and incident response.
  • Insight into innovation workflows at PLDT Innolab and how R&D informs products.

Jairosoft Inc.

Details

Jairosoft Inc. showcased professional Agile teams composed of Sitecore-certified architects, developers, and project managers.

The company demonstrated Agile/SCRUM workflows, use of TFS, and their transition toward SAFe for large-scale enterprise projects.

Takeaways

  • Learned that they are an AI First at Jairosoft.
  • Observed Agile/SCRUM practices and team collaboration at Jairosoft.
  • Understanding career roles: architects, developers, project managers.
  • Best practices for code review and continuous delivery pipelines.
  • Networking and professional conduct in a software company setting.
